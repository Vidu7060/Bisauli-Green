Farmer Sourcing

As a Farmer Producer Organization (FPO), we work directly with our member farmers to collectively grow, aggregate, and supply agricultural produce. Our sourcing model is designed to empower farmers by ensuring fair pricing, transparent operations, and shared value creation. By eliminating unnecessary intermediaries, we strengthen farmer livelihoods while maintaining quality, traceability, and sustainability across the supply chain.

Farmer Sourcing Includes:
- Direct sourcing from FPO member farmers
- Aggregation of produce at village and cluster level
- Quality checks at farm and collection points
- Promotion of organic and chemical-free farming
- Support in organic and quality certifications
- Fair, transparent, and collective pricing system
- Timely and secure payments to member farmers
- Reduced dependence on middlemen
- Training on quality standards and post-harvest handling
- Complete traceability from farm to buyer
- Collective bargaining power for farmers
- Long-term farmer empowerment and income stability
